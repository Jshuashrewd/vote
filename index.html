Electronic voting

E lectronic voting is a system of voting which tackles riots during election. It also increases free and fair election and also there will be no form of rigging or manipulation. This system of voting brings us into a digitalized world of voting which takes care of casting and counting ballots. Electronic voting makes voting easier for us by bringing voting into the comfort of our homes. Electronic voting also reduces the stress and expenses of counting ballots which if counted by a machine makes it more accurate. it increases integrity, security, accuracy, swiftness, privacy, auditability, cost-effectiveness, and also ecological sustainability.
